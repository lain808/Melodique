Melodique is a tool to generate melodic phrases procedurally based on random chord progressions. It is intended to help those who wish to learn about chord progressions and the basics of compositions as well as those looking for new melody ideas. The controls at the top of the page can be used to adjust various parameters affecting the way in which the melodic phrases are generated.
